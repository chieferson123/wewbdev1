Our History

It all began in 2013 with the start of a new Mass time at the parish of St. Francis de Sales in Ajax, Ontario, Canada.

Over 8 years, numerous wonderful singers, multiple collaborations with other talented choirs in our Parish, 3 albums and 100+ videos later, the Sunday 7pm Choir has evolved into an international YouTube Catholic & Christian choir with over 50,000 subscribers, 8 Million views and counting.

More importantly, we continue to have the honour of serving the congregation and praising the Lord through song at St. Francis de Sales Parish.

Our Mission

From Chant, Traditional Hymns, Taizé and SATB choral anthems, to Contemporary, Praise & Worship, Gospel and Spiritual songs, we strive to sing beautiful sacred, Christian choral music from all genres, all for the glory of the Lord.
